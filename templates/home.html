How it works

  1. You indicate your roommate preferences
  2. We check if another user's preferences match yours
  3. If yes, we send you an email with the other user's preferences
  4. You can message the other user to discuss in more detail

How we try to help

  1. We match
  2. We provide a rudimentary messaging service so you do not need to disclose any contact information (including ITSC) to prospective roommates until you are comfortable with that
