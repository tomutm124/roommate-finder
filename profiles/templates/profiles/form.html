Instructions:

  1. Most of the fields below are optional, you do not need to fill all of them if you do not want to
  2. The "Required" checkbox: If you mark a field as "Required", we will only show you profiles that match yours on that field. This can significantly reduce the number of matching profiles you have
  3. The Weight: Alternatively, you can leave the "Required" button unchecked and indicate a weight. Any profile that matches yours on that field will get that amount of score. You can then indicate a minimum score. Only profiles with a total score at least that will be shown to you
  4. Any match in the Languages you provide is considered as a match on the language field
  5. Bedtime means the time you go to bed, not the duration of your sleep. Any overlap in bedtime is considered as a match for that field
  6. If you leave a field empty, its weight will not contribute to the total score
  7. If you mark a field as required, its weight will not contribute to the total score
  8. The Allow Unmatched Users to Contact You checkbox: Some times your profile matches someone else's preferences but their profile does not match yours. Checking this button allows these users to send you a message (we will let you know by email after you get a message). They will be able to see your profile, but not your ITSC
  9. We provided some default values of weights for you. Please feel free to change them

Tips:

To maximize your chance to find a match, please try to:

  1. Fill in as many fields as possible
  2. Mark fewer (or no) fields as "required"
  3. Set a lower "Minimum Total Score" (remember unmatched 9am classes cause score deduction)
  4. Check the "Allow Unmatched Users to Contact You" box to give other people a chance to discuss with you. You might make a new friend :)
